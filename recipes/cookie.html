Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Vanilla
  • Quick Oats
  • Peanut Butter

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Combine sugar, butter, cocoa powder and milk in a saucepan
  3. Cook over medium heat and whisk constantly until it reaches a boil
  4. Let boil for 1 minute undisturbed
  5. Remove from heat and add vanilla, peanut butter, and oatmeal
  6. Stir
  7. Create cookies by dropping rounded spoonfuls onto a plate
  8. Refrigerate cookies so they can set
