Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet italian sausage
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 teaspoons basil
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  3. Cook sausage over medium heat until browned
  4. Stir in tomato sauce and basil
  5. Cover and simmer for 2 hours, stirring occasionally
  6. To assemble, spread sauce in the bottom of 9x13 inch baking dish
  7. Layer noodles, cheese, and sauce
  8. Cover with foil
  9. Bake for 25 minutes
  10. Remove foil and bake for another 25 minutes
